FAQs

FAQ

How can I renew my insurance policy?

You can renew your insurance policy in two ways online or offline.
The online renewal process is super quick and simple and takes only a few minutes.

Is it possible to renew bike insurance online?

Yes, it is possible to renew your bike or Two wheeler Insurance online. The process is a simple 2-step process.

All you have to do is visit our website and provide the details of your previous policy. You will receive a quote, after which you can make the payment. The entire process can be done within minutes.

What documents do I need to renew my insurance policy?

We dont believe in complicated processes and you only need 2 documents to renew your policy:
A copy of the previous insurance policy
Certificate of registration

What are the benefits of a long term policy?

Longer coverage period: You will only have to renew once in two or three years, depending upon the coverage period you choose
Added Protection: Protection from third party premium rate hikes and service taxes which occur almost every year
Added NCB benefit No Claim Bonus will not be cut down to nil if you make a claim during the policy period. It will be lowered, but it will still remain valid
Proportional Refund You can get a proportional refund in case of cancellation of policy, even if you made a claim during the policy period

How much time does it take for my insurance policy documents to arrive?

The hard copy of your Two Wheeler Insurance will be sent to your address via courier. It usually takes up to 7-10 days to reach you. However, you will get a soft copy via email as soon as the payment is verified, and the policy is issued.

You can also find the policy document on our website's customer portal using your issuance number. Additionally, you can access your account through our Mobile App, Insurance Wallet, or get updates through our twitter page, using our #TweetInsurance service.

What happens if my Two Wheeler Insurance expires?

If your Two Wheeler Insurance policy has expired, then you become exposed to the risks you were insured against. Some of these negative effects are: Your NCB is negatively affected

You are exposed to third party liability risks

Cost of repairs for your two wheeler will increase as they are no longer covered by an insurance policy

However, you dont need to fret. You can renew your Two Wheeler Insurance Policy even after its expiry and continue enjoying the benefits. You just need to do it within 30 days.

Add on Covers

We all like something extra, dont we? Bajaj Allianz Car Insurance provides a host of add-on covers to supplement your car insurance policy. Our add-on covers include:

  • Loss or Damage Due to Natural Calamities

    Natural calamities are the most unpredictable thing out there, other than the behaviour of teenagers, perhaps. The unexpected occurrence of a natural calamity and the destruction in its wake is exactly the kind of situation where Two Wheeler Insurance comes handy. Your insured vehicle is covered against fire, explosion, self-ignition or lightning, earthquake, flood, typhoon, hurricane, storm, tempest, inundation, cyclone, hailstorm, frost, landslide and rockslide.

  • Loss or Damage Due to Man-made Calamities

    Man-made calamities throw light on the dark side of humanity and are just as devastating and unpredictable as natural calamities. We dont quite have superheroes saving the day, but our comprehensive Two Wheeler Insurance policy does help better handle the aftermath. Our Bike Insurance policy covers burglary, theft, riot, strike, malicious act, accident by external means or any terrorist activity. Additionally, any damage in transit via rail, road, inland waterway, lift, elevator or air is also covered by the insurance policy.

  • Personal Accident Cover

    When were protecting your vehicle, how can we not protect you? Our Two Wheeler Insurance policy comes with a Personal Accident cover of Rs 15 lakh for the owner or driver. It covers accidents that may occur while driving the vehicle, travelling on it and mounting or dismounting from it. You can also add Personal Accident cover for a pillion rider with this policy to offer the same protection to your loved ones.

  • Third Party Legal Liability

    Someone elses mistake should not harm you and likewise you should not be the cause of someone elses loss. Hence, third party liability cover is mandatory in India, to make everyone take responsibility for their actions. We offer Two Wheeler Insurance policies that take care of this statutory requirement for you and cover liability payable as third party property damage or loss. This covers any damage, loss, death or bodily harm caused to another person or property by the insured vehicle.
